A brief history of Royal Polytechnic College

About us

Royal Polytechnic College (RPC) is a co-educational, self financing polytechnic established in the year 2007 by MMIRT Educational Trust under the dynamic leaderships of Founder Chairman Mr.KRN.BOSE B.sc, and Trustee Members, Mr.KRN.Ramesh Tmt K.Indurani, , B.PANDISELVI. R.RADHA .This polytechnic has been approved by Government of TamilNadu and All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi and Affiliated by Directorate Technical Education, chennai. The infrastructural facilities have been provided at optimum level for effective instruction. The Institution is situated at Kulathur on the National highway between Pudukkottai and Trichy at a distance of 18 km from Pudukkottai Near by Keeranur.

Vision

To redefine the scope of higher education by infusing into each of our pursuits, initiatives that will encourage intellectual, emotional, social and spiritual growth, thereby nurturing a generation of committed, knowledgeable and socially responsible citizens.

Mission

Ensure State of the world learning experience

Espouse value based Education

Empower rural education

Instill the spirit of entrepreneurship and enterprise

Create a resource pool of socially responsible world citizens.

Motto

"Rural upliftment through Technical Education"
.
Objectives

To impart High Quality Education and create an atmosphere most conducive for the pursuit of knowledge.

To create a World Class Engineering Institution for the benefit of the students and community at large.

Chairman Message

I feel privileged to welcome you all to our website.

Until early 2007, higher education remained only a dream in the minds of rural Indian students and they had to go a long way in fulfilling their aspirants. Considering this plight and to help the unreached, MMIRT Educational & Charitable Trust was formed in the year 2007.This Trust is now running from the year 2018 under the visionary leadership of KRN.BOSE . Royal Polytechnic College was established in the year 2007. with Four Diploma Courses.

Royal Polytechnic College is run by MMIRT Educational Trust, established with Philanthropic motive by its Chairman Thiru KRN.BOSE, B.Sc, who has served as a Contractor over the past four decades. Basically a hard and smart worker and a man with helping tendency. Royal polytechnic college is a creation to impart and provide such a knowledge which is strength and power to the society with the learning of the latest developments of the world to meet the growing demand for trained man power. We are set to take the mission of implementing new education methodologies to enable quality learning. It also encourages independent thinking and helps the student in developing wholesome personality so that they can contribute their best to the society and the country. Royal polytechnic college takes keen interest in updating it's infrastructures which provides an ambience that stimulate intellectual thinking and academic interation to meet the technological revolution and new challenges of modern era.
