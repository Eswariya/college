Admission Procedure

01
Xth Standard or equivalent passed

Passed 10th Std./ SSC examination

Obtained at least 35% marks in the qualifying examination

02
For lateral Entry

Chemistry as compulsory subjects along with Mathematics/ Biology subject. OR 10+2 Science (with Mathematics as one of the Subject) or 10+2 Science with Technical Vocational subject. OR 10 th + (2 years ITI) with appropriate Trade in that order shall be eligible for admission to Second Year Diploma Course(s) of appropriate

03
Students Admissions

Conditions For Admissions

Candidates are admitted for the diploma programme at different entry levels according to their entry qualification. Age limit and entry qualification will be followed as per the government of Tamilnadu admission rules from time to time.
