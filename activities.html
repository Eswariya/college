Alumni

Aims and Objectives

To imbibe the vision of the Society

To create close bonds of friendship and support between the institutions and the former students.

To encourage fellowship among the alumni themselves in order to sustain them to live out their professional lives inspired by the values received in our institutions.

To become deeply involved in the problems of the city in order to make life more just – even getting involved in the political sphere.

To grow in faith that does justice, to become men and women for others and to manifest a particular concern for the poor.

Nss

N.S.S. a centrally sponsored scheme was introduced in 37 selected universities on 24-09-1969 both "as a measure of Educational Reforms and as a mean to improve the quality of educated man power". It was extended to plus two schools in 1985. The scheme has aroused among the student youth an awareness of the reality of life, a better understanding and appreciation of the problems of the people. It is an attempt in making education relevant to the needs of the society.

Aim

The overall aim of the scheme is to "Arouse The Social Consciousness Of The Students And To Provide Them With Opportunity To Work With People In The Villages And Slums, And To Expose Them To The Realities Of Life" so as to "bring about a change in their social perceptions".

Regular NSS Activities

Under this our college students undertake various programs in adopted villages and urban slums during week-ends or after teaching hours. Volunteers are required to put in minimum of 120 hours of community service, orientation and campus work in accordance with the NSS Manual and instructions/guidelines of Govt. of India. Volunteers may be placed in the following projects under community work.

Physical Education

Sports Activities

"A sound mind makes a sound body”. As to this saying, sports activity plays a vital role. The physical education shapes the individual physically, mentally, socially and morally. The college believes in promoting health and physical fitness through sports and games. The college has a good sports field and sufficient space for training students in athletics and major games like basketball, volleyball, football, cricket etc. Students have ample scope for participation at College, inter College, divisional and state events. Besides these, the physical education department also conducted various intramural competitions for staff and students.

Our Polytechnic College is a member of Inter polytechnic Athletic Association for Sports and Games. Special coaching is given to identified students to drive them up to participate in divisional and state level competition

Physical Education department provides facilities for all major games, like Volleyball, Basketball, Football, Cricket, Badminton, Hockey, Tennis and other indoor games. There is a well maintained track for races and athletic events. Gymnastic equipments are also provided for aspiring students.

The polytechnic takes part in all tournaments and sports conducted by Inter-Polytechnic Athletic Association and Clubs. Our Polytechnic continues to be the winners in the Inter Polytechnic Volleyball matches.
