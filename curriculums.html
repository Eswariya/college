Theory sessional marks

The theory sessional marks will be having two components:

a. Internal assessment ( Assignments,Seminars,Attendance)

b. Model examination

The internal assessment will be divided in the following components with weightings:

a) Attendance – 5marks

b) Assignments – 5 marks

c) Seminor – 5 marks

d) Internal test – 5marks

e) Model test – 5marks
