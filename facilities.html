Infrastructure

Royal Polytechnic College (RPC) is a co-educational, self financing polytechnic established in the year 2007 by MMIRT Educational Trust under the dynamic leaderships of Founder Chairman Mr.KRN.BOSE B.sc, and Trustee Members, Mr.KRN.Ramesh Tmt K.Indurani, , B.PANDISELVI. R.RADHA .This polytechnic has been approved by Government of TamilNadu and All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi and Affiliated by Directorate Technical Education, chennai. The infrastructural facilities have been provided at optimum level for effective instruction. The Institution is situated at Kulathur on the National highway between Pudukkottai and Trichy at a distance of 18 km from Pudukkottai Near by Keeranur.

Hostel

Separate Hostel facilities are provided for Boys only Hostels are provided with good recreational, communication facilities, play courts, indoor games etc. Round the clock security system functions for the safety of the inmates

Rules

No student is admitted without submitting the application for admission

Admission into the hostel is made for every year.

Mess charge per month will be collected based on dividing system

Prior permission should be obtained from the deputy warden or tutor if an inmate wishes to stay away from hostel during late night

Absence from the hostel without getting permission will be viewed seriously

The hostel gate will remain closed from 7.30pm to 6.00am

Loss or damage of electrical and other fittings are common to all students of the block

Strict discipline should be maintained within the hostel campus

The inmates of the hostel should not bring any day scholars or strangers into the hostel at any time

Library

All working days 9.30AM TO 4.30PM

Rules

Students should enter their names in the entry register

Other than library books, no other items should be taken to the library

Students should have their ID cards

Strict silence should be maintained in the library

Only 2 books will be issued to the students at a time

Books should be returned within 15 days

For the delayed period a fine of 1 rupee per day will be collected

Advance reservation is required for some special books

Reference books and periodicals will not be allowed to be taken outside the

In case of loss of books, the students should inform it immediately to the Librarian

Books should be handled carefully

Reading periodicals in groups should be avoided

All books should be returned before examinations

The library should be kept clean and tidy

The corners of books should not be folded

The pages must not be torn

Nothing should be written in the library books

The personal money or letters are not to be kept in the books

Transport

The college has a fleet of 10 buses that ply from vantage points in and around trichy, aranthangi and other places to the campus and back to facilitate comfortable commuting for both students and staff. The buses are manned by drivers who are well trained and experienced in safe driving practices.

Lab Equipments

The college has provided all best and modern equipments for best coaching to students.

Sports

Our Polytechnic College is a member of Inter polytechnic Athletic Association for Sports and Games. Special coaching is given to identified students to drive them up to participate in divisional and state level competition
